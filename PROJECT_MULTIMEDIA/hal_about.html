~ABOUT US~

"Green Side" communities are communities formed with the purpose to planting trees as an effort to preserve the environment
and share knowledge and experiences on how to plant trees or other plants that can be practiced by community members.
This community was founded in 2018 which is processed independently by bringing peoples who have the same thoughts
and have the will to make changes.Communities hold activities to replant trees and carry out other activities
such as gardening and make travel plans to doing missions while build relationships between members.
